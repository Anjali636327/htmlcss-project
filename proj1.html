A Mother's Love :
In every moment, big or small, Your love embraced and held us all. With gentle hands and caring smile, You made our lives so worthwhile.
Your wisdom shines in every word, A voice of comfort that's always heard.
Through joy and tears, you're always there, A guiding light, beyond compare. With tender heart and endless grace, You fill our lives with warm embrace.
Your love, a gift that's pure and true, A tribute to all that you do.
So here's to you, our guiding star, A mother's love, both near and far. For all you are and all you do, This tribute is our thanks to you.
